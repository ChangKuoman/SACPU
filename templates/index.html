SACPU - PC BUILD SIMULATOR

LOGIN REGISTER

Our project is a web application made with the Flask micro Framework that allows the simulated purchase of a computer taking into account the motherboard and its compatibility with the different components as the base piece. The information in the database is based on different web pages for the purchase of specialized technology in computers such as LoginStore, and proprietary brands such as MSI, AMD, Intel, among others.
